Our Core Philosophy: Elevating Minds, Building Builders

Sankalp Digital Pathshala (founded in 2020 by the Sankalp Shiksha Foundation) stands as a beacon of academic excellence and technical innovation in Salemgarh, Tamkuhi, Kushinagar, Uttar Pradesh. Our institute was established to prove that high-end technical training and academic rigour are not exclusive to metropolitan areas. We believe every rural student possesses the analytical capability to master coding syntax, construct robotics units, analyze sensor data, and excel in board examinations when given identical learning facilities.

Our academic strategies are designed around active learning. Rather than passive note-taking, our students participate in interactive smart-classroom discussions, write and compile code projects, and wire microcontroller boards in our NextGen labs. We aim to equip students with both the logical skills required for school boards and the computational proficiency demanded by modern industries.

Our Journey

Milestones & Transformations

How a small coaching initiative evolved into Kushinagar's primary technology-driven learning ecosystem.

2020

Founding & Core Classes Launch

Launched in Salemgarh with 50 students, offering core sciences and mathematics preparation for UP Board secondary classes. Set up our first smart board classroom.

2022

Founding of NextGen Electronics Workbench

Introduced basic digital literacy modules. Partnered with regional tech mentors to construct a small hardware lab equipped with Arduino microcontrollers and sensor blocks.

2024

AI Learning Modules & Competitive Exam Classes

Expanded enrollment to 500+ students. Initiated integrated JEE & NEET preparation courses alongside python programming, prompt engineering, and visual diagram solvers.

2026 (Present)

India's Leading Rural Future-Skills Hub

Featuring dedicated robotics workbench bays, drone kinematics simulators, advanced computer classrooms, and an integrated AI Student Counselling system serving the entire Tamkuhi region.

Our Core Pillars

  • Rural Transformation: Direct technical upliftment by equipping rural students with future-ready skills.
  • Pedagogical Integrity: Top educators providing comprehensive math and science conceptual blueprints.
  • Hands-on Labs: Moving beyond books. Students build, wire, program, and deploy applications.
  • Ethics & Leadership: Helping students leverage technologies responsibly to address regional challenges in agriculture and transport.
