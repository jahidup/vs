Transformative Outcomes

From Salemgarh to Global Tech Readiness

Explore case studies of rural students from Kushinagar who secured top board rankings, qualified for JEE/NEET, and built functional technology projects.

Case Study 01

Aditya Pratap Singh: Python Coding To District Topper

Background: Aditya joined our secondary program with basic digital literacy, lacking standard computer exposure. Under our integrated program, he balanced standard UP Board Science formulas with Python algorithms and microcontroller programming.

Outcomes: Aditya secured a district-level high score of 98.2% in class 10 boards while building a functional solar tracking array in the hardware lab. His story proved that technical exposure enhances analytical math performance.

"The interactive smart boards and coding challenges at Sankalp completely resolved my math blockages." - Aditya
Case Study 02

Priyanjali Maurya: Securing 99.4 Percentile in JEE

Background: Hailing from a remote village near Tamkuhi, Priyanjali wanted to crack national level engineering exams without relocating to costly city hubs. She received a 100% tuition scholarship under the Sankalp Shiksha Foundation.

Outcomes: With daily structured tutoring, advanced board prep, and All India Mock practice drills, Priyanjali scored 99.4 percentile in JEE Main, securing entrance opportunities at top NITs.

"Sankalp gave me urban-grade mentoring right at home, saving my family from massive relocation bills." - Priyanjali
Case Study 03

Vikram Kushwaha: Securing 645 Marks in NEET

Background: Vikram wanted to pursue medicine but struggled with complex biological concepts and organic chemistry equations during his high school classes.

Outcomes: Guided by our Ph.D science mentors, Vikram utilized interactive visual screens to explore molecular structures. He secured 645 marks in NEET, qualifying for state medical universities.
