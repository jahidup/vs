Integrated Board + Tech Mentorship Method

At Sankalp Digital Pathshala, we believe teachers must serve as practical mentors. Rather than traditional chalk-and-talk lectures, our team uses digital interactive screens, circuit diagrams, and interactive simulator code blocks. Our educators maintain deep academic connections with regional boards (UP Board and CBSE) while maintaining industry-level coding awareness in web design and hardware operations.

Our weekly faculty workshops review student progress, construct mock tests, customize study materials, and ensure every student gets focused personal guidance. Parents can schedule weekly counseling sessions with our instructors to evaluate their child's academic and technical growth.
