Salemgarh's Advanced Learning Environment

Take a walkthrough of our physical and digital campus setups, designed to match the classroom standards of metropolitan institutions.

Smart Classrooms & Interactive Boards

Our classrooms are equipped with high-definition digital smart boards. This allows mentors to display 3D models of chemical bonds, project geometric coordinate transformations, and run simulations for complex physics concepts. Rather than static textbook diagrams, students learn through interactive visual tools.

  • High-definition touch-interactive boards.
  • 3D visual models for science classes.
  • Seamless stream integration for guest lectures.

High-Speed Computer & AI Labs

Our computer laboratory provides access to modern processing terminals and high-speed internet. This is where students write and compile code, interface sensor kits, and access AI chatbot helpers. The lab is open to all students for coding practice and research.

  • Linux & Windows terminal workstations.
  • High-speed broadband fiber connections.
  • Safe internet filters for younger students.

Technical Library & Collaborative Zones

A quiet study and reference zone housing board textbooks, JEE/NEET manuals, robotics publications, and programming guidebooks. It includes a collaborative discussion area where students work on group code bases and design hackathon prototypes.

  • Extensive repository of competitive textbooks.
  • Collaborative study tables and whiteboards.
  • Digital tablets to access our E-book libraries.

Counseling & Career Guidance Cell

A private counseling room where students align their academic interests with technical career options. Mentors host weekly alignment sessions with parents, detailing student board results, python test scores, and university roadmap plans.

  • Private counselor tables.
  • Custom academic roadmap worksheets.
  • Comprehensive university entry guides.
