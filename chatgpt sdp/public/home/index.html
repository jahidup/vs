Bridging the Digital Divide: Salemgarh's Educational Renaissance

Sankalp Digital Pathshala was founded in 2020 with a single, clear objective: to bring elite, urban-grade education to the doors of students in Salemgarh, Tamkuhi, Kushinagar, Uttar Pradesh. For decades, students from rural communities faced a stark choice: relocate to distant cities at immense financial strain, or accept basic, traditional schooling. We built SDP to bridge this gap, integrating standard CBSE & UP Board state curriculums with modern technical courses.

Our methodology does not stop at conventional whiteboard lectures. We believe that a student in Kushinagar has the capacity to build the next major software application, program drone models, or write algorithms, provided they are given access to high-speed internet, smart digital classrooms, and hands-on laboratory kits. By combining core science, mathematics, and humanities with AI, robotics, coding, and Internet of Things engineering, we are turning rural dreams into global technical achievements.

Our Pillars of Excellence

A New Standard of Learning

Traditional coaching teaches memorization. We foster curiosity, technical competence, and logical reasoning.

Academic Integrity

Expert board preparation covering Classes 6-12 (Science, Commerce, and Humanities). We employ structured micro-tests, mock boards, and question analysis to ensure students master board concepts and score top marks.

NextGen Robotics & IoT Lab

Our dedicated robotics lab teaches students microcontroller programming using Arduino. Students write C/C++ scripts to control sensor networks, build mobile applications, and assemble remote quadcopter models.

AI Learning & Coding Center

We train students in Python programming, machine learning methodologies, and prompt engineering. Our students use advanced tools to build custom software, solve real math graphs, and build web portfolios.

Structured Career Guidance

Through logical roadmaps, we help students navigate career avenues in software engineering, medical sciences, public administrations (IAS/PCS), artificial intelligence research, and entrepreneurship.
